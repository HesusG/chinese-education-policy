## Summary Paragraph

The Ministry of Education runs China's AI education system from top to bottom. It sets curricula at every school level and has pushed every major AI education directive since 2018, starting with the AI Innovation Action Plan that created "AI+X" programs at universities and expanded doctoral enrollment. In late 2024, the MOE told all K-12 schools to teach AI across every grade and picked 184 schools as pilot bases. By September 2025, every primary and secondary school in China had to offer at least 8 hours of AI instruction per year.
