Steadily Progressing Toward the Strategic Goal of Building an Education Power! Interpretation of the 2024 Top 10 National Education News  

1  
National Education Conference Held, Charting the Course and Providing Fundamental Guidance for Building an Education Power  

2  
Education System Continues to Strengthen Ideological and Political Education, Making Positive Progress in Constructing a New Framework for Fostering Virtue and Talent  

3  
Outstanding Teachers Honored on the 40th Teachers’ Day; CPC Central Committee and State Council Issue "Opinions on Promoting the Spirit of Educators and Strengthening the Construction of a High-Quality Professional Teaching Workforce in the New Era"  

4  
China International College Students’ Innovation Competition Reaches New Heights in Quality, Highlighting Shift Toward "Capability and Quality-First" Talent Cultivation Model  

5  
National Smart Education Platform Forms "Four Horizontal, Five Vertical" Resource Framework, Entering a New Stage of High-Quality Digital Education Development  

6  
Degree Law and Preschool Education Law Adopted, Marking Major Breakthroughs in China’s Education Legal System  

7  
Launch of the Basic Education Standardization and Management Year Initiative to Expand Quality Education and Better Meet the Public’s Aspiration for "Good Education"  

8  
First National University Regional Technology Transfer Center Established, Kicking Off a New Era in Promoting University Scientific and Technological Achievements Transfer  

9  
2024 World Vocational Education Development Conference Successfully Held, Showcasing China’s Advanced Concepts and Successful Practices in Vocational Education  

10  
Steady Progress in High-Level Education Exchanges and Cooperation, Significantly Enhancing China’s International Education Influence
