Notice of the General Office of the Ministry of Education on Announcing the List of Artificial Intelligence Education Bases for Primary and Secondary Schools  

Jiao Ji Ting Han 〔2024〕 No. 5  

Departments of Education (Education Commissions) of all provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government; Education Bureau of Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps:  

In accordance with the requirements of the "Notice of the General Office of the Ministry of Education on Recommending Artificial Intelligence Education Bases for Primary and Secondary Schools" (Jiao Ji Ting Han 〔2023〕 No. 29), and following the processes of selection and recommendation by provincial-level education administrative departments and centralized public announcement by the Ministry of Education, 184 artificial intelligence education bases for primary and secondary schools have been finalized. The list is hereby announced (see attachment for details).  

Attachment:  
List of Artificial Intelligence Education Bases for Primary and Secondary Schools  

General Office of the Ministry of Education  
February 19, 2024
