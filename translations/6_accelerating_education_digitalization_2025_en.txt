Opinions of the Ministry of Education and Eight Other Departments on Accelerating the Advancement of Education Digitalization

**Opinions of the Ministry of Education and Eight Other Departments on Accelerating the Advancement of Education Digitalization**

Jiao Ban [2025] No. 3

In order to implement the "Education Power Construction Plan Outline (2024–2035)," use education digitalization as a key breakthrough, open up new tracks for education development and shape new advantages for development, and fully support the construction of an education powerhouse, the following opinions are proposed.

**I. Overall Requirements**

Adhere to the guidance of Xi Jinping Thought on Socialism with Chinese Characteristics for a New Era, thoroughly implement the spirit of the 20th National Congress of the Communist Party of China, the Second and Third Plenary Sessions of the 20th Central Committee, and the National Education Conference, fully implement General Secretary Xi Jinping's important expositions on education, especially the important instructions on education digitalization, deeply implement the national education digitalization strategy, adhere to application orientation and governance as the foundation, uphold connection as the priority, content as the essence, and cooperation as the key, focus on integration, intelligence, and internationalization, expand the beneficiary scope of high-quality educational resources, promote the empowerment of education transformation by artificial intelligence, accelerate the formation of a ubiquitous and accessible lifelong education system, assist in building a learning society where everyone can learn, everywhere is capable of learning, and learning is possible at all times, and provide strong support for effectively responding to the new round of technological revolution and industrial transformation and accelerating the construction of an education powerhouse.

In the work, it is necessary to: adhere to fostering virtue through education, firmly grasp the correct political direction and value orientation, and enhance the ability of online education. Adhere to application orientation, leading high-quality education development through deep application. Adhere to digital empowerment, promoting holistic changes in educational philosophy, teaching models, and education governance. Adhere to a people-oriented approach, focusing on cultivating students' higher-order thinking, critical thinking, and practical abilities. Adhere to reform and innovation, actively conform to the development trends of new technologies such as artificial intelligence, and improve the institutional system adapted to digital development. Adhere to overall planning, pilot first, strengthen inter-departmental coordination, central-local linkage, and regional coordination, and encourage social participation. Adhere to safe development, building a credible and controllable security barrier. Adhere to open cooperation, deepen international exchanges, and enhance the global influence of China's digital education.

**II. Deepen Integration, Build and Utilize the National Smart Education Public Service Platform**

**(1) Improve the "Four Horizontal and Five Vertical" Platform Resource Layout of the National Smart Education.** Use the National Smart Education Public Service Platform (hereinafter referred to as the National Platform) as a hub to integrate high-quality platforms, resources, and services at all levels, gradually achieving unified entry, resource sharing, and data integration. Build and gather high-quality resources around the four major fields of basic education, vocational education, higher education, and lifelong education, and the five major sections of moral, intellectual, physical, aesthetic, and labor education. Encourage localities, schools, and industry enterprises to leverage their advantages to develop high-quality resources. For basic education, build high-quality curriculum resources covering national curriculum textbooks and adapted to different learning situations, as well as science education, culture, and art resources. For higher education and vocational education, build high-quality digital courses covering various disciplines, virtual simulation practice, thesis and practical achievement resources, etc. Increase the supply of resources for ideological and political education, physical education, aesthetic education, labor education, special education, and language. Build lifelong education resources covering family education, social education, elderly education, and vocational skills enhancement. Enhance the platform's intelligence level, increase platform openness, innovate new forms of resources, enhance resource interactivity, and achieve personalized intelligent recommendations. Improve the full lifecycle management mechanism for resource development, launch, application, evaluation, and withdrawal. Establish resource evaluation standards, conduct dynamic evaluations by level and category, improve expert assessment and user feedback mechanisms, implement a digital resource inclusion and collection certificate system, promote resource refinement, systematization, and specialization, and build a high-quality resource supply ecosystem.

**(2) Continuously Upgrade the Public Service Functions of the National Platform.** Promote "efficiently handling one thing," and expand the "one-stop online services" for education public services. Optimize services such as enrollment, examination evaluation, student status inquiry, academic degree certification, teacher qualification inquiry, and Mandarin proficiency certificate inquiry. Optimize the National College Student Employment Service Platform to provide high-quality services for college student internships, employment, and industry enterprise recruitment. Optimize the full online service for studying abroad to enhance convenience.

**(3) Promote Deep Application of the National Platform Across the Entire Domain.** Develop work guidelines, clarify the key work priorities and promotion mechanisms at the provincial, municipal, county, and school levels, formulate application strategies according to the characteristics and laws of different educational stages, and enhance application effectiveness. Promote the formulation of digital solutions for overall regional education equity and high-quality development in each province (autonomous region, municipality), achieving interconnection between provincial platforms, resources, services, and the National Platform. Deeply implement initiatives such as "Attending the Same High-Quality Class Together," MOOC Westward 2.0 Plan, and Reading Action, with targeted support for rural areas, ethnic regions, and poverty-alleviated areas. Promote the regular application of "Dedicated Delivery Classroom," "Famous Teacher Classroom," and "Famous School Network Classroom," and expand high-frequency scenario applications such as lesson preparation and teaching, homework management, class management, assessment and evaluation, home-school communication, and after-school services. Deepen the construction of online studios for famous teachers and improve the online teaching research mechanism.

**(4) Promote Education Data Integration and Effective Governance.** Build the National Education Big Data Center well, unify data standards and interface standards, build a cross-level, cross-region, cross-department education data sharing network, and smooth data circulation. Promote the interconnection of education data with national population, spatial geography, economic society, industry, and other data. Strengthen data integration, connect the full-chain management information systems of schools, students, and teachers, gradually achieve "one data, one source," deeply mine the rich ore of education data, and build a new system of big data-enabled education governance.

**(5) Accelerate the Construction of a Lifelong Learning Public Service System.** Strengthen the digital infrastructure for a learning society and promote the construction of a ubiquitous and accessible lifelong education system. Accelerate the introduction of qualification framework standards, build a national credit bank, and promote the vertical and horizontal integration of academic education and non-academic education. Accelerate the establishment of a learning outcome certification mechanism, and explore the establishment of a lifelong learning academic degree and diploma granting mechanism based on the credit system. Build the lifelong education section of the National Smart Education Platform well. Accelerate the construction of a new form of National Digital University, and explore new mechanisms for online non-academic and academic education credit certification and degree granting. Improve the National Open University system and build the National Open University for the Aged well.

**III. Comprehensively Promote Intelligence, Facilitate AI-Driven Educational Transformation**

**(6) Strengthen Forward-Looking Layouts in AI and Other Areas.** Accelerate the construction of large-scale AI education models. Improve the multimodal corpus in the education field and build high-quality, independently controllable datasets. Strengthen algorithm security assessments to ensure correct value orientation. Plan a number of forward-looking research topics, conduct AI application pilots in an orderly manner, explore new paradigms for "AI+Education" application scenarios, and promote the deep integration of large models with education and teaching. Promote vertical applications of large models in ideological and political education, science education, aesthetic education, mental health, and other fields, as well as in basic disciplines such as mathematics and physics, and cultivate an application ecosystem.

**(7) Promote Digital Upgrading of Disciplines and Majors and Reform of Research Paradigms.** Facing the development of the digital economy and future industries, optimize the setting of higher education disciplines and majors, plan ahead for digital-related disciplines and majors, and integrally promote talent cultivation, technological innovation, technology R&D, and achievement transformation. Facing the digital transformation needs of advanced manufacturing and modern services, dynamically adjust vocational education majors, empower deep integration of industry and education, and serve the "One Body, Two Wings" construction and the cultivation of high-skilled talents. Use AI technology to drive research paradigm reform, improve research organization efficiency, enhance achievement transformation efficiency, derive new growth points for disciplines, and assist in building a number of emerging and interdisciplinary disciplines.

**(8) Promote Digital Transformation of Curriculum, Teaching Materials, and Teaching.** Improve knowledge graphs, build capability graphs, deepen the application of large education models, promote the intelligent upgrading of curriculum systems, teaching material systems, and teaching systems, integrate AI technology into all elements and the entire process of education and teaching, and promote the integration of science education and humanities education. Coordinate the integration of AI education across primary, secondary, and higher education, build "general + specialized" AI general education courses in universities, build a number of smart courses in universities, properly offer information technology-related courses in primary and secondary schools, and encourage the offering of specialized AI courses. Formulate guidelines for the construction and management of digital teaching materials, develop a number of exemplary high-quality digital teaching materials by field and major, and support localities, schools, and enterprises in developing digital teaching materials. Explore the construction of cloud schools, smart manufacturing spaces, and future learning centers, build "AI+X" national experimental teaching centers, create new teaching organizational forms, and promote changes in learning methods. Build "one-stop" digital-intelligent student communities. Explore new models of human-machine collaborative teaching through intelligent learning companions, digital tutors, etc., achieve AI-driven large-scale personalized education, and improve the efficiency and quality of education and teaching.

**(9) Focus on Teachers and Students to Enhance the Digital Literacy and Skills of the Entire Population.** Deeply implement the action outline for enhancing the digital literacy and skills of the entire population, and improve network civilization literacy and digital ethics. Formulate and improve digital literacy standards for teachers and students and AI application guidelines, carry out practical activities and survey assessments for literacy enhancement, and improve digital literacy and AI application levels. Establish a connected digital literacy cultivation system across primary, secondary, and higher education, and incorporate digital literacy into comprehensive quality evaluations. Deepen the action of AI-assisted teacher team building, and integrate digital literacy into teacher education curriculum systems. Establish a rotation training system to improve the digital literacy of education management cadres and school administrators.

**(10) Fully Support Education Decision-Making and Governance.** Accelerate the construction of the "Education Digital Map" to support trend prediction, planning decisions, and risk warnings. Build a prediction and warning model for basic education school places to support the optimization of education resource allocation. Build a national talent supply and demand big data platform to support the dynamic adjustment and optimization of major layouts, discipline settings, and enrollment scales, and promote high-quality full employment for graduates. Build a national discipline big data information resource database and establish a big data-based discipline development monitoring system. Improve the digital and information management capabilities of education finance and strengthen the supervision of education fund usage.

**(11) Empower Education Evaluation Reform.** Establish an education evaluation mechanism supported by big data and AI, improve outcome evaluations for different entities such as schools, teachers, and students, and conduct multi-dimensional process evaluations, value-added evaluations, and comprehensive evaluations. Promote the accompanying data collection of the entire teaching process and all development elements, and conduct precise profiling. Strengthen the orientation of comprehensive development education, and promote digital empowerment of examination and evaluation reform. Promote digital pilots for examinations such as higher education self-study exams. Achieve data sharing for university teaching, research, management, and services, and promote the digital transformation of institution, discipline, and major evaluations.

**IV. Vigorously Promote Internationalization, Continuously Enhance the International Influence of Digital Education**

**(12) Promote International Co-construction and Sharing of Digital Education Resources.** Establish multilateral, multi-level digital education international cooperation dialogue mechanisms and build a digital education international cooperation system. Build the international version of the National Platform well, enrich international course resources, strengthen connections with relevant countries and international organization platforms, and explore the establishment of country-specific sections. Continuously implement the "MOOC Going Global" action and promote the co-construction of high-level courses with renowned foreign universities. Empower the "Luban Workshop" and other vocational education going global projects, and rely on overseas vocational education institutions, university overseas learning centers, and enterprise overseas training centers to cultivate "digital + skills" composite talents for developing countries. Enrich Chinese digital learning resources, build the Chinese Alliance well, and enhance the coverage of international Chinese education.

(13) Building a Globally Influential Digital Education Brand. We will continue to host the World Digital Education Conference, the World MOOC and Online Education Conference, and the International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Education. We will establish and utilize the World Digital Education Alliance and the World MOOC and Online Education Alliance. We will operate an international journal on digital education, select global exemplary cases of digital education, and regularly publish the China Smart Education Blue Book, the Global Digital Education Development Report and Index, and the World Higher Education Digital Development Report and Index. These efforts aim to create a world-class model for the digital transformation and development of education.

(14) Empowering the International Cultivation of Talent. We will cooperate in building digital courses, digital training bases, and virtual laboratories, promote joint international talent cultivation, and strengthen open science and technology collaboration. We will establish a mutual recognition mechanism for digital learning credits, conduct pilot programs for mutual recognition of digital academic qualifications between the National Digital University and foreign higher education institutions, and explore new pathways for talent cultivation. We will establish overseas learning centers for digital education, with a focus on providing digital technology capacity training for developing countries. We will jointly build international think tanks for digital education and cultivate talent for international think tanks.

(15) Actively Participating in Global Digital Education Governance. We will proactively engage with international organizations related to digital education and actively cooperate with UNESCO, the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD), the G20, BRICS, the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation, and others to promote the establishment of an international community for digital education development. We will actively participate in setting international agendas, rules, and standards for digital education, and promote the adoption of international consensus on standards for the National Smart Education Platform, digital literacy of teachers and students, and digital education security and ethics.

V. Improving the Digital Education Support System

(16) Enhancing Infrastructure. We will actively utilize national support policies such as the "Two New" initiatives to upgrade digital education infrastructure. We will promote the allocation of public network, computing power, and cloud resources toward educational applications. We will establish mechanisms for sharing computing resources among regions and higher education institutions. We will advance the standardized construction of smart campuses and gradually普及 intelligent teaching terminals. We will promote the large-scale deployment and application of IPv6, encourage primary and secondary schools to reasonably expand their export bandwidth to meet educational needs, and guide schools to support network infrastructure construction, gradually achieving full wireless network coverage on campuses. We will accelerate the construction of the education dedicated network, explore the development of an education industry cloud, and orderly promote the migration of educational applications to the cloud.

(17) Improving the Standardization System. We will accelerate the formulation and revision of digital education standards, establishing a comprehensive system of standards and norms covering the software and hardware environment, platform tools, digital resources, educational data, and network security of digital education. We will strengthen the support of educational platforms for the mandatory national standard of Chinese coded character sets. We will develop standards for platform management and service quality assurance. We will promote the organic integration of digital education standards from national, local, industry, enterprise, and group sources.

(18) Establishing a Diversified Investment Mechanism. Adhering to the principle of public welfare, we will leverage the leading role of the government and establish a diversified investment mechanism involving the government, society, and enterprises. We will ensure funding for new types of educational infrastructure construction and the purchase of high-quality digital resources and services, with preferential support for rural and remote areas as appropriate. Basic telecommunications enterprises will offer discounted network usage fees for schools at all levels and of all types. We will utilize various channels, including market financing, to guide social capital in supporting digital education development. Schools will strengthen fund coordination to ensure expenditures on digital education. We will build a nationwide unified market for the supply of digital educational resources, guide enterprises in developing digital education products and solutions that meet application needs, and protect the intellectual property rights of resource contributors.

(19) Establishing an Application Evaluation and Incentive Mechanism. We will adhere to an application-oriented approach, conduct tiered and categorized evaluations of the effectiveness of digital education construction and application, and incorporate these into school operational level assessments. We will include digital education projects in the National Teaching Achievement Awards and make digital application a prerequisite for applying for national education and teaching awards, incorporating it into school and teacher recognition criteria. We will publish an annual national report on digital education.

VI. Strengthening the Security Barrier for Digital Education

(20) Ensuring High-Quality Operation of Key Platforms. We will enhance the support capacity of critical information infrastructure and platform systems. We will strictly enforce resource content review systems, implement platform accountability, and adhere to the principles of reviewing all provided, launched, and updated content to ensure political correctness, proper orientation, scientific validity, applicability, standardization, timeliness, and public benefit. We will establish a multi-departmental coordination mechanism for key periods and regularly conduct security assessments and inspections.

(21) Building a Network Security Protection System. Leveraging the National Public Service for Network Identity Authentication, we will establish a trusted identity and data system in the education sector, strengthening real-name management. We will fully implement security protection for the entire lifecycle of educational data, enhancing capabilities to prevent tampering, leakage, and misuse of core and important data. We will strengthen the protection of minors' personal information and conduct compliance audits for personal information protection. We will fully implement the network security responsibility system and ensure network security等级保护 (classified protection).

(22) Strengthening Artificial Intelligence Security Assurance. We will establish a security assurance system for "Artificial Intelligence + Education". We will implement the filing mechanism for artificial intelligence algorithms and large models, explore the establishment of an algorithm security evaluation system, and effectively avoid issues such as network attacks, information cocoons, algorithm hegemony, and dependency addiction. Adhering to the digital ethics principles of being people-oriented and ensuring that AI is used for good, we will strengthen supervision of intelligent education products, tools, and services, and standardize the management of AI applications in schools.

VII. Strengthening Organization and Implementation

We will improve the leadership system, operation and maintenance mechanisms, and evaluation mechanisms for digital education. We will establish a cross-departmental coordination mechanism: the education department is responsible for formulating the overall plan and specifically promoting implementation; the cyberspace administration and public security departments are responsible for strengthening the supervision of ideology, network security, data security, personal information protection, and the development and application of information technology in the digital education field, creating a healthy and positive online environment; the development and reform and finance departments are responsible for supporting digital education projects and funding; the science and technology department is responsible for strengthening research布局 (layout) in key areas and using AI technology to promote changes in research paradigms; the industry and information technology and data management departments are responsible for promoting the construction of digital education infrastructure and data interoperability; the human resources and social security department is responsible for formulating the national qualifications framework and协同 (coordinating) promoting the construction of a lifelong education system. All localities and schools should treat digital education as a top-leader project. Provincial education departments should strengthen overall implementation, conduct systematic training and leadership training, enhance cognitive and executive capabilities, formulate implementation plans based on local conditions, boldly conduct pilot programs, actively develop personalized resources, promote innovative applications, and explore new pathways for digital education to promote equitable and high-quality educational development, as well as new teaching methods and talent cultivation models. We will establish an expert advisory committee for digital education to strengthen research and guidance. We will build a high-quality, professional team for digital education management and technical support. We will promote and publicize the effective practices and experiences of various localities and schools.
